FAQs

What type of devices can I trade in?

The Dell Trade-In service accepts Dell and non-Dell electronic devices such as computers, laptops, and tablets. This program does not accept household appliances or TVs. If your device does not qualify, Dell recycles it for free.

How much do I get for my trade-in?

Several attributes are considered to determine the eligibility and amount of the Dell Trade In credit, such as age, make and model, health, and physical state of the device.

How can I use my Dell Trade-In credit and where?

Your Dell Trade-In credit is eligible for purchase of products or services on Dell.com.

How do I prepare my device?

Delete your data and remove your passwords. Here's help on how to erase the data on your device.

If my device is not eligible for credit, how can I recycle it for free?

You receive an email confirming the device can be recycled with Dell for free. Pack it in any suitable box and take it to a FedEx Office near you. Dell covers the cost of shipping.
