Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our blog. We want to keep things simple and easy to understand.

Information We Collect

When you browse our website, we collect basic, non-personal data. This includes things like the type of browser you're using, how long you stay on the site, and which pages you read. We do this to understand what content is most helpful so we can write better articles.

Cookies and Tracking

Like almost every website, we use cookies. Cookies are tiny text files saved on your device that help the site remember you. We use cookies to keep the website running smoothly and to display relevant advertisements.

Advertising and Third Parties

To keep this website free, we show ads. We partner with third-party advertising networks, such as Monetag, to display these ads. These partners may use cookies to show you ads based on websites you've visited in the past. We don't share your personal name or email with these ad networks.

Your Rights (GDPR Transparency)

Even if you live outside of Europe, we believe in the principles of the GDPR. You have the right to know what data is collected and how it's used. Since we don't require you to create an account, we don't hold personal data like your home address or phone number. If you have concerns about the basic analytics we collect, you are always free to clear your browser cookies or use an ad-blocker.

If we ever change how we handle privacy, we'll update this page so you always know what's going on.
